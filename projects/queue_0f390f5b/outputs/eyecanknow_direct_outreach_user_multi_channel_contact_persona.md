## Who Is This Person

**Role:** business professional managing operations across multiple channels  
**Context:** A business owner or professional who has been burned by unreliable software and poor support experiences. They actively research across multiple channels and provide feedback when products fail to meet their operational needs, but their patience for technical issues is extremely limited.  
**Tech savviness:** medium | **Price sensitivity:** high | **Risk tolerance:** low

A user contacted via phone, email, LinkedIn, Trustpilot, or Google reviews. Provides feedback inconsistently depending on response rates. Interaction is dependent on engagement and often limited.

## Trigger Map

| Trigger Type | Specific Conditions |
|---|---|
| **Converts when** | Clear pricing with no hidden fees visible upfront / Live chat or phone support responds within minutes / Software installs and runs without any glitches during trial |
| **Abandons when** | Support page only shows FAQ or chatbot options / Software crashes or bugs appear during evaluation / Pricing requires signup to view or has unclear terms |
| **Trusts when** | Support team demonstrates actual technical knowledge / Pricing breakdown is detailed and transparent / Software works seamlessly across required platforms |
| **Suspicious when** | Free trial requires credit card for basic features / Support responses are generic or automated / App requests excessive or undisclosed permissions |

### Deal Breakers (Immediate Exit)

- 🚫 Software that crashes or freezes during initial evaluation
- 🚫 No human customer support available
- 🚫 Hidden fees discovered after purchase
- 🚫 Mobile app lacks key features compared to desktop version
- 🚫 Installation failures or excessive bugs during setup
