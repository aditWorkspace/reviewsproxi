## Anti-Patterns (What NOT to Do)

Common mistakes when designing experiences for this demographic:

- ⚠️ Assuming they prioritize flashy features over reliability - they need proven stability first
- ⚠️ Focusing on cost savings alone - they value operational continuity over minor price differences
- ⚠️ Treating them as new users - they have specific technical requirements based on current systems
- ⚠️ Highlighting ease of use without addressing integration complexity - they need technical depth
- ⚠️ Rushing the evaluation process - they require thorough technical validation before committing
