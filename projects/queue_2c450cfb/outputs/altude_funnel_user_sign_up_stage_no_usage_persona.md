## Who Is This Person

**Role:** Tech-cautious individual seeking reliable tools without complexity  
**Context:** Someone who signs up for products after outreach but gets overwhelmed by technical issues, setup complexity, or hidden barriers before actually using the product. They abandon quickly when faced with bugs, unclear pricing, or friction that wasn't apparent during the sign-up process.  
**Tech savviness:** medium | **Price sensitivity:** high | **Risk tolerance:** low

A user reached through outreach (e.g., Telegram groups) who expresses interest and signs up for the product. Does not proceed to actual usage or integration after sign-up. Behavior is observed through funnel drop-off rather than direct feedback.

## Trigger Map

| Trigger Type | Specific Conditions |
|---|---|
| **Converts when** | Clear pricing is displayed upfront without signup required / Mobile app has positive reviews mentioning stability / Setup process promises to be simple with minimal steps |
| **Abandons when** | App crashes during initial setup or first use / Login process fails repeatedly or shows network errors / Pricing becomes unclear or hidden costs appear |
| **Trusts when** | Product works immediately after signup without configuration / Mobile and desktop versions have feature parity / Clear guidance is provided for any setup steps |
| **Suspicious when** | App requests more storage or permissions than expected / Accounts disconnect without explanation or warning / Product feels gamified rather than professional |

### Deal Breakers (Immediate Exit)

- 🚫 Persistent mobile app crashes making it unusable
- 🚫 Complete inability to log in or connect accounts
- 🚫 Accounts frequently disconnect without warning requiring repeated re-linking
- 🚫 App crashes, network errors, or failure to load content
- 🚫 Feeling cheated by hidden costs or unfair algorithms

- ⚠️ Hiding pricing behind signup walls - contradicts their frustration with unclear pricing and transparency needs
- ⚠️ Emphasizing gamification elements - contradicts their annoyance with apps feeling like games instead of serious tools
- ⚠️ Requiring extensive onboarding - contradicts their need for immediate usability without complex setup
- ⚠️ Promoting mobile-desktop feature differences - contradicts their expectation for core feature parity across platforms
