## Who Is This Person

**Role:** Busy professional seeking efficiency tools but overwhelmed by complexity  
**Context:** Time-constrained individual who signs up for tools seeking quick solutions but abandons when faced with complex setup or unclear value. They want immediate results with minimal effort but consistently encounter friction that prevents actual usage.  
**Tech savviness:** medium | **Price sensitivity:** high | **Risk tolerance:** low

A user reached through outreach (e.g., Telegram groups) who expresses interest and signs up for the product. Does not proceed to actual usage or integration after sign-up. Behavior is observed through funnel drop-off rather than direct feedback.

## Trigger Map

| Trigger Type | Specific Conditions |
|---|---|
| **Converts when** | Product demonstrates immediate value without setup / Clear pricing with no hidden costs or complexity / Interface appears simple and intuitive at first glance |
| **Abandons when** | Setup process becomes complex or technical / Multiple configuration steps required before use / Technical glitches occur during onboarding |
| **Trusts when** | Pricing and features clearly displayed upfront / Product works exactly as advertised immediately / Setup process is genuinely simple and fast |
| **Suspicious when** | Features are locked behind upgrade prompts / Setup requires more technical knowledge than advertised / Product seems more complex than initially presented |

### Deal Breakers (Immediate Exit)

- 🚫 Requires technical setup or installation beyond simple account creation
- 🚫 Hides pricing or features behind mandatory signup process
- 🚫 Shows complex interface with multiple configuration options on first use
- 🚫 Demands credit card information before allowing product testing
- 🚫 Fails to work immediately without additional setup steps
