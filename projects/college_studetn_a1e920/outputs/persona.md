## Trigger Map

| Trigger Type | Specific Conditions |
|---|---|
| **Converts when** | Sees 'Amazon's Choice' badge on affordable option / Finds detailed negative review with manufacturer response / Product video shows actual college student using item |
| **Abandons when** | Sees 'product arrives defective' in multiple recent reviews / Checkout requires account creation / Shipping timeline exceeds 5 days |
| **Trusts when** | Reviews mention 'lasted 2+ semesters' / Brand offers student discount / Clear warranty terms in product description |
| **Suspicious when** | Only 5-star reviews with generic praise / Price seems too good to be true / Product photos look stock/edited |

### Deal Breakers (Immediate Exit)

- 🚫 Multiple reviews mentioning 'product breaks quickly'
- 🚫 No clear warranty information
- 🚫 Requires proprietary accessories not included
- 🚫 More than 15% 1-star reviews
- 🚫 Shipping costs exceed 10% of product price
