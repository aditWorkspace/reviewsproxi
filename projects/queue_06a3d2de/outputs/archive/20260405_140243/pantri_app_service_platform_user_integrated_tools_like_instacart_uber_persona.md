## Trigger Map

| Trigger Type | Specific Conditions |
|---|---|
| **Converts when** | Recommendation from others or positive reviews / Clear evidence of accessible customer support / Transparent pricing with no hidden fees |
| **Abandons when** | App glitches and technical failures during initial use / Hidden or unclear pricing structures / Poor communication during service failures |
| **Trusts when** | Platform shows clear tasker vetting processes / Customer support responds quickly to test inquiries / Pricing remains consistent between browsing and checkout |
| **Suspicious when** | App interface shows bugs or broken functionality / Pricing requires signup to view details / Customer support contact information is hard to find |

### Deal Breakers (Immediate Exit)

- 🚫 App crashes or has login/password issues during critical tasks
- 🚫 Hidden or unclear fees that weren't disclosed upfront
- 🚫 Complete lack of responsive customer support when problems occur
- 🚫 Inability to communicate with service providers before booking
- 🚫 Platform algorithms that feel opaque and uncontrollable
