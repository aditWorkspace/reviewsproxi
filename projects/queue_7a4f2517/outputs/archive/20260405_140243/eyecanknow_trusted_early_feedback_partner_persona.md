## Trigger Map

| Trigger Type | Specific Conditions |
|---|---|
| **Converts when** | Clear evidence of software reliability and stability in reviews / Responsive customer support with specific examples of problem resolution / Transparent pricing with no hidden fees or forced upgrade paths |
