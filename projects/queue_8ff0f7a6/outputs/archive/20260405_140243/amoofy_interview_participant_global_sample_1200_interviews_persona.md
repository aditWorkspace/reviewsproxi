## Trigger Map

| Trigger Type | Specific Conditions |
|---|---|
| **Converts when** | Finds recommendation from trusted source with similar health conditions / Sees specific health need addressed with clear ingredient transparency / Discovers cost-effective solution with proven track record |
| **Abandons when** | Encounters excessive cost without clear benefits / Finds mention of severe negative side effects / Discovers dissatisfaction with current alternatives in reviews |
| **Trusts when** | Sees effective symptom management results in detailed reviews / Finds convenient and easy-to-use products that fit daily routines / Discovers affordable, reliable wellness solutions without dependency risks |
| **Suspicious when** | Pricing information is hidden or unclear / Health claims seem too good to be true without scientific backing / No mention of potential side effects or interactions |

### Deal Breakers (Immediate Exit)

- 🚫 Severe negative side effects mentioned in reviews or warnings
- 🚫 Excessive cost without clear benefits or proven effectiveness
- 🚫 Products that worsen existing conditions like anxiety or histamine reactions
- 🚫 Lack of reliable delivery options for essential health products
- 🚫 No clear information about ingredients or potential interactions with existing conditions

5. Compares multiple cost-effective alternatives before making health-related purchases

7. Avoids products that require significant energy investment to use or maintain

9. Validates product claims against trusted medical sources or community recommendations
